About Auburnable

Auburnable was founded with a simple mission: to integrate modern technological advances with everyday life. Made up of two high school students in Silicon Valley, we aim to make life both easier and more advanced for all through this endeavor. We were founded by a passionate high school student, musician, and coder who came up with a product that returns music you like from other genres, instantly. Our Co-CEOs finalized this product and deployed it in August 2016, and have been working tirelessly to develop new products to ease your life.
